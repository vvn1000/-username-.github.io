Concept

For my ISP project I made a word search. The word search scrambled every time you refreshed the page, it
had letters that could be clicked on, and a counter that tracked how many words you found. This allowed the game to
be played multiple times with different outcomes.

Motivation

I decided to make a word search because it was one of the games I played the most on my phone when I was
younger. Being able to make my own version of one of my most frequently played games was very fun and rewarding in a way.

Code Segments

The "onEntityMouseClick" function takes the x and y coordinates of the point at which you clicked.
It then takes the coordinate point and finds what letter is stored on the word search grid at that point.
Finally, it highlights the letter you clicked on. This code creates the 15 x 15 2D array. The array is made of
blank characters so that later on in the code the blank characters can be replaced with letters. This function
goes through the grid and checks if each spot is blank. If the spot is blank, it places a random letter from the
alphabet and moves on to the next. If the spot already has a letter stored in it, it moves on to the next. Before
this function runs, a function to randomly place words on the grid is run.

Conclusion

The finished game ended up working and was playable, but there were definitely parts that could be improved.
One part that could be fixed would be the selecting system. Because of time constraints, I wasn't able to make it to
where you could click and drag to select a word. Instead, you have to click each individual letter in the word you
want to check. Once you have a whole word selected, you press the letter "s" on your keyboard to check the selected word
against the key. Another idea I didn't get to add was a randomized word bank. With additional time, I would've added more
words to the word bank and then randomized the words that got chosen. The final part that could be improved is the word bank
that is displayed on the players screen. When you find a word in the game, the word turns green on the word search but
doesn't get crossed out from the word bank. With additional time, I would've added that feature into the game.
